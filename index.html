PARSA

"No handrails here, just an expression of pure abstract dynamic rendered in a perpetually struggling flux of sharp and distorted textures and fidelities, discombobulated arrhythmia and atonality.
But lets not get it twisted, there’s an impish playfulness in these hyper chromatic convolutions that definitely shouldn’t be blithely titled ”noise”,
but rather exist according to their own logic of synaesthetic sensations and baffling calculations that makes everything else seem a bit prissy and stupidly uptight by comparison"

PARSA (b.1992) is a Tehran-based computer musician. You can reach him via somedeepname at gmail dot com.
